## Icon Design Guidelines

### Color Scheme
- **Primary**: NSMessenger Purple (#3F1879)
- **Accent**: Secondary Purple (#6E3DB8)
- **Background**: White or gradient

### Design Options
1. **Simple NS Monogram**: "NS" letters in purple
2. **Chat Bubble**: Purple speech bubble with "NS"
3. **Communication Icon**: Stylized message/connection icon

### Design Requirements
- **High contrast** for visibility
- **Simple design** that works at small sizes
- **No text** except brand initials
- **Corner radius** will be applied by iOS automatically
